DialMaster — Auto Dialer CRM

A privacy-first auto-dialer assistant for sales teams — imports contacts, opens the device dialer, and stores user data locally by default.
OAuth & Data Privacy Policy Terms & Conditions

What DialMaster does (short)

Import contacts (.csv/.xlsx) or paste numbers, map fields, then run a calling session — each call opens the phone's native dialer (ACTION_DIAL). After each call, record outcome & notes. Local storage by default; encrypted cloud backup is opt-in.

OAuth – exactly what we request

Requested scopes (explicit):

ScopeWhy we request it
openidSecure authentication and unique identifier (required).
emailLink account to purchases, subscriptions and support.
profileShow user's name and avatar in the app (UX only).

Important reviewer note: DialMaster does not request Gmail, Google Contacts uploading, Google Drive, or other restricted scopes. There is no call recording, no automated call log uploads, and no background audio capture.

Data Safety — short summary

What we collect: Google account id, email, display name for authentication; telemetry for analytics & ads; purchase tokens for subscription verification. What we do not collect/upload by default: device call audio, full contact uploads, SMS, or health data.
