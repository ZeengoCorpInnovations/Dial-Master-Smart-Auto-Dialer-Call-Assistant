A lightweight auto-dialer CRM that opens your device's native dialer for each contact. Privacy-first: contacts & notes stay on the device.
Google Sign-In: email, profile, openid OAuth details

What DialMaster does

DialMaster automates call sequencing for outreach teams while ensuring user control: every call is launched through the device's native dialer (ACTION_DIAL). Users import .csv/.xlsx or paste numbers, confirm mapping, and start a calling session—after each call the app prompts for outcome & notes. The app stores contact data locally on device (Room DB).

Key actions & policies

  • Google Sign-In required (one-time online) to map purchases & subscriptions.
  • Contacts and notes are stored on the device only; cloud backup is opt-in and encrypted.
  • Ads present for free users; premium removes ads. Rewarded ads grant coins after server verification.
  • No background call logging, no call recordings, no call log uploads.
